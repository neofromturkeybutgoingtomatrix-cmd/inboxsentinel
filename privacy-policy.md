# Privacy Policy for InboxSentinel

**Last updated: May 2026**

InboxSentinel reads your Gmail DOM (what's visible on screen) to analyze email sender patterns. No data leaves your browser.

- **No OAuth:** InboxSentinel never accesses your Gmail account or Google credentials
- **No server:** All data is stored locally in chrome.storage.local
- **No tracking:** Your email content is never read, stored, or transmitted
- **No third parties:** Data is never sold or shared

**What we read (from the visible page only):**
- Sender names and domains
- Email subjects (for pattern analysis only)
- Dates (for recency tracking)

**What we NEVER access:**
- Email body content
- Attachments
- Your Google account credentials
- Your contacts
